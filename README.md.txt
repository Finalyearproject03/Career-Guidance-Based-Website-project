About This Project
This is a Career Guidance Based Website project. The project includes career opportunities, an online test to suggest the best career, and an online e-book reader section. The system may be used to automate the workflow of the company recruitment process and their approvals. It gives opportunity to student apply for jobs online and also keeps track of all students details. The company can upload job offers and they can view job applications. Even this project stores student profile information with bio-data, computer skills, and educational qualification details. The project is designed to create an Online based guidance tool that would help students explore and make decisions about their career strands. It provides information about occupations, Training programs, and educational programs for students. This project will also be able to provide students find the right career by giving them a chance to explore education and training options, as well as build a portfolio using the internet. The administrator can maintain records of students activity reports, Occupation reports, practice tests, training programs, etc. With project:- Report, PPT, Class Diagram, Setup Your Device, All Doubts are Clear, Viva questions, Etc.
